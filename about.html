About TransformVXR

Through relational intelligence, immersive learning, and real-time AI coaching, we help professionals build the human skills that drive performance, including the ones no automation can replace.

  • Real-time, relational coaching

Ellie Kazemi

CEO, TransformVXR

For years, Ellie worked in conflict resolution, helping people navigate tough conversations and bridge the gaps between good intentions and mutual understanding. Across roles with supervisors, students, and leaders, she saw the same pattern: communication and perspective gaps that were not about motivation, but about connection.

As a behavioral scientist, professor, and founder of TransformVXR, Ellie combines learning science and technology to help leaders build stronger, more connected workplaces. Over 20 years, she has served in executive roles including Chief Science Officer, and partnered with organizations like NASA and FEMA on training, measurement, and workforce development.

She created TransformVXR to make relational skills accessible and scalable, blending immersive AI simulations with a framework for relational intelligence that strengthens engagement, communication, and collaboration across teams.

Jonas Acquah

CFO, TransformVXR

Jonas is a real estate investor and the CFO of TransformVXR, combining strategy, finance, and systems thinking to help organizations scale through stronger workplace relationships. His background spans investment banking, capital markets, and enterprise leadership, including roles as Divisional CFO at JPMorgan Chase, Head of Center of Excellence for Technology Finance at Chubb, and Head of Business Management for Liquidity Solutions at Silicon Valley Bank, a division of First Citizens Bank.

A first-generation Ghanaian American, Jonas is grounded in community, faith, and the belief that business success is inseparable from human connection.

What We Believe

The principles we work from

Workplace relationships drive business performance

When automation takes over tasks, human connection becomes your edge. Empathy, communication, and ethical leadership are strategic, not just soft skills.

These skills can be taught and practiced

Relational skill is not a fixed trait. Like any behavior, it can be shaped through structured practice, immediate feedback, and repetition with increasing difficulty.

Real-time coaching leads to lasting change

Awareness does not change behavior. Fluency-based practice does. We build tools that create behavior change, not just insight.

We practice what we teach

We believe in continuous learning, reflection, and feedback. Growth starts with us. We hold ourselves to the same standard we hold our clients.
