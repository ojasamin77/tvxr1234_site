The Problem

Clinically excellent.
Relationally underprepared.

BCBAs are trained to analyze behavior, write plans, supervise implementation, and make clinical decisions. But many are never given a safe place to practice the conversations that determine whether that work succeeds: parent pushback, RBT accountability, ethical pressure, and difficult feedback.

The result is a gap between knowing what should happen clinically and knowing how to say it when the relationship is at stake.

01
Parent trust is breaking down

When clinical progress is being affected by a breakdown in the family relationship dynamic, making the conversation feel impossible to start.

02
Clinical judgment is under pressure

When the ethical path and the organizational expectation point in different directions. The stakes affect both the client and the career.

03
An RBT is drifting from the plan

When data quality issues and protocol drift require a direct supervision conversation, and knowing how to have it without losing the relationship.

Why this is not "just communication training."

These conversations affect treatment follow-through, staff consistency, parent trust, escalation risk, and whether clinical recommendations actually turn into action. They are not soft skills. They are clinical performance moments.

Most CEUs teach what to know. TransformVXR helps you practice what to say.

Instead of watching another lecture, you enter a realistic conversation, respond in your own words, and receive feedback on the observable behaviors that moved the interaction forward, or held it back.

  • ✗ No passive watching.
  • ✗ No generic "good job" feedback.
  • ✗ No practicing for the first time in the real conversation.
How It Works

Real conversations. Real responses.
Real behavioral feedback.

Behavioral skill acquisition requires practice under realistic conditions with immediate, specific feedback. TransformVXR gives BCBAs a safe way to rehearse the conversations that usually happen with no rehearsal at all.

Enter the scenario

A responsive avatar presents a realistic ABA situation. The context feels familiar, but the stakes are safe.

Have the conversation

You respond in real time, in your own words. No scripts. No multiple choice. The avatar responds to what you actually say.

See what your communication did

Feedback is based on what you actually said, not what you clicked. Receive feedback on observable behaviors such as open questions, reflective statements, interruptions, pacing, and missed opportunities to repair or reframe.
