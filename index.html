The Problem

Clinically excellent.
Relationally underprepared.

ABA training produces skilled clinicians. It does not produce clinicians who have practiced the conversations that determine whether that clinical work actually lands. The parent who pushes back. The RBT who is not following the plan. The director who keeps adding to your caseload. These conversations happen every week. Most BCBAs face them without ever having practiced.

Why It Matters

These are not soft skill gaps. They are the conversations that determine whether your clinical work succeeds, whether your team trusts you, and whether your clients stay enrolled. Most BCBAs have never had a safe space to practice them.

The Transform Difference

Practice in a private, avatar-based environment. No colleagues watching. No career on the line. Behavioral feedback that tells you exactly what moved the conversation forward and what held it back.

How It Works

The mechanism behind
real skill change.

Behavioral skill acquisition requires practice under realistic conditions with immediate, specific feedback. Reading about a conversation does not change how you have it.

Meet your AI avatar

A realistic, responsive avatar presents the clinical scenario. The situation is real. The stakes are safe. No colleague is watching.

Have the conversation

You respond in real time. No scripts, no multiple choice. The avatar responds to what you actually say. Your authentic behavioral repertoire shows up, and that is what gets measured.

Get behavioral feedback

Feedback is specific and behavioral. Not "you did well." Instead: which observable behaviors moved the conversation forward and which escalated it. Grounded in ABA measurement principles.
