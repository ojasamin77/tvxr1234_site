Foundations of High-Stakes Communication

What separates effective clinical communicators from those who avoid hard conversations is not personality. It is behavior. This module establishes the functional framework underlying all communication skill.

  • The behavioral anatomy of a difficult conversation
  • Identifying your own avoidance patterns and triggers
  • Setting up the conditions for productive exchange
  • First AI practice session with immediate feedback
