RIQ Foundation

How people relate determines how organizations perform

The RIQ to PIQ framework is the intellectual foundation behind every Transform product. It explains why relational capability is not a soft skill, but a measurable driver of organizational outcomes.

Why Relational Capability Comes First

Performance is rarely limited by technical skill alone

Modern work and care environments are defined by complexity. In these conditions, performance is shaped by trust, communication, psychological safety, and relational clarity.

These are not personality traits. They are capabilities, and they can be measured, developed, and strengthened.

As automation and AI take on more technical work, the skills that differentiate people are increasingly human. Relationship Intelligence integrates emotional intelligence and durability skills, focusing on how these show up between people in real interactions.

  • Cross-functional collaboration
  • Emotional labor
  • Ethical decision-making
  • High-stakes conversations
  • Rapid change and uncertainty

What Is Relationship Intelligence (RIQ)?

RIQ is the ability to create trust, psychological safety, and connection in real interactions, especially when stakes are high.

High RIQ does not mean being agreeable or nice. It means being clear, present, responsive, and relationally aware, even in difficult moments.

RIQ shows up in moments such as:

  • Giving and receiving feedback
  • Navigating conflict or disagreement
  • Supervising and supporting others
  • Communicating under stress
  • Engaging families, clients, or customers with care
What RIQ Measures

Observable behaviors, not personality

Through assessment and guided practice, RIQ focuses on the specific behaviors that make relational dynamics visible so they can be practiced and improved, not left to chance.

How people listen and respond

Whether someone is truly present, curious, and attentive in a conversation, or managing the surface while disengaging underneath.

How safety and trust are built or broken

The specific behaviors that signal psychological safety to others and those that inadvertently undermine it, even with good intentions.

How power and authority are exercised

Whether authority is used to create alignment and accountability or to create compliance and avoidance.

How emotions are acknowledged and regulated

The difference between suppressing affect and genuinely regulating it in a way that keeps the conversation productive.

How clarity is maintained under pressure

Whether people can stay specific, grounded, and outcome-focused when a conversation becomes emotionally charged.

The Framework

From RIQ to PIQ

Relational capability is not the end goal. It is the foundation. When relational skills improve, they unlock Performance Intelligence: the measurable impact of how people work together over time.

Relationship Intelligence

RIQ

The observable behaviors that create trust, psychological safety, and connection in real interactions under pressure.

→
Performance Intelligence

PIQ

The measurable impact of relational capability on how teams collaborate, how organizations retain people, and how outcomes are delivered.

  • Stronger collaboration
  • More effective supervision
  • Faster issue resolution
  • Reduced escalation and conflict
  • Workforce resilience and retention
  • Consistent quality of care, service, or delivery
How Transform Operationalizes It

A system, not a theory

Transform is designed to move organizations from insight to action across five connected steps.

Measure relational capability

Validated assessments reveal how communication, trust, and psychological safety are experienced across roles and teams.

Identify patterns and risk

Organizations can distinguish between individual skill gaps and systemic relational breakdowns, and prioritize accordingly.

Develop targeted pathways

Insights inform role-specific learning, leadership development, and certification pathways aligned to real organizational needs.

Practice and coach at scale

AI-enabled coaching and immersive practice allow relational development to scale consistently without losing nuance or human context.

Track growth over time

Organizations gain longitudinal insight into relational capability and performance impact. This is how RIQ becomes PIQ: not through intention, but through structure.
