How It Works

Ten minutes in, you'll understand why practice changes everything

Here is exactly what happens when you use TransformVXR, step by step, with no marketing in the way.

The Experience

From first login to behavioral fluency

1

Choose the scenario that fits your situation

You start by selecting the type of conversation you need to practice. The library covers the full range of high-stakes BCBA interactions: parent resistance, staff performance, caseload negotiation, clinical disagreements with leadership, and more. Or you can describe your own situation in plain language and the AI will build a scenario around it.

Scenario selector
Parent pushback on recommendations RBT corrective feedback Caseload negotiation Data integrity conversation Managing staff burnout Custom scenario
2

The AI plays the other person. You respond.

The AI takes on the role of the parent, staff member, or supervisor. It does not give you an easy version of the conversation. It pushes back, deflects, escalates, and redirects the way people actually do. Your job is to respond in real time, using whatever language comes naturally.

Live session
Parent
I've been doing this for three years and I've never seen any real progress. I don't think this program is working.
I understand how frustrating that is. Can we look at the data together so I can show you what's changed?
3

You receive precise feedback after every exchange

After each response you give, the AI analyzes what worked, what missed, and why. Feedback is framed in behavioral terms, not generic coaching language. You will see exactly where your response de-escalated the situation, where it inadvertently reinforced the other person's resistance, and what to try differently.

4

Repeat, vary, and build fluency

Run the same scenario again. Change the severity. Try the other end of the emotional spectrum. Each repetition is tracked. Over time you will see clear data on where your responses have improved and where you still hesitate. This is behavioral rehearsal with the same logic you apply to your clients.

Session history
Attempt 1 Escalated early
Attempt 2 Improved de-escalation
Attempt 3 Clinical limit maintained
5

Earn certification by demonstrating skill

When you are ready, you can take a certification assessment. It is a live roleplay evaluation scored across multiple dimensions. Pass, and you receive a credential with CEU credit that reflects what you can actually do. It is not awarded for completing modules. It is earned by performing well under pressure.
